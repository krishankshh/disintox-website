# Certification & Approval Logos

## Required Logo Files

Upload the actual logo files to this directory (`/public/images/approvals/`) with the exact filenames listed below.

### Logo Specifications

| Organization | Filename | Format | Recommended Size | Notes |
|-------------|----------|--------|------------------|-------|
| **WHO** (World Health Organization) | `who.svg` or `who.png` | SVG (preferred) or PNG | Min. 200x200px | Transparent background preferred |
| **US EPA** (Environmental Protection Agency) | `epa.svg` or `epa.png` | SVG (preferred) or PNG | Min. 200x200px | Official EPA logo |
| **FDA** (Food and Drug Administration) | `fda.svg` or `fda.png` | SVG (preferred) or PNG | Min. 200x200px | Official FDA logo |
| **FSSAI** (Food Safety Standards Authority of India) | `fssai.svg` or `fssai.png` | SVG (preferred) or PNG | Min. 200x200px | FSSAI official logo |
| **ISO 9001:2015** | `iso.svg` or `iso.png` | SVG (preferred) or PNG | Min. 200x200px | ISO certification mark |
| **NSF** (National Sanitation Foundation) | `nsf.svg` or `nsf.png` | SVG (preferred) or PNG | Min. 200x200px | NSF International logo |

## File Requirements

- **Format**: SVG is preferred for crisp scaling, PNG is acceptable
- **Size**: Minimum 200x200 pixels for PNG files
- **Background**: Transparent background is preferred
- **Color**: Full color logos (the website will apply grayscale effect automatically)
- **Quality**: High resolution, official logos only

1. **WHO**: https://www.who.int/about/policies/publishing/logo
2. **US EPA**: https://www.epa.gov/web-policies-and-procedures/epa-seal-and-logo

5. **ISO**: From your ISO certification provider
6. **NSF**: https://www.nsf.org/
